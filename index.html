Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical session last?

Executive sessions typically last 60-90 minutes, including wardrobe changes and multiple poses. We ensure ample time to capture the perfect shots while maintaining a relaxed atmosphere.

What should I wear to my session?

We recommend bringing 2-3 professional outfits in solid colors. Dark suits, blazers, and professional attire in navy, charcoal, or black work best. We'll provide detailed guidance before your session.

How quickly will I receive my photos?

Initial proofs are available within 48 hours, with final retouched images delivered within 5-7 business days. Rush delivery is available for time-sensitive needs.

Do you offer hair and makeup services?

Yes, we partner with professional stylists and makeup artists who specialize in camera-ready looks. These services can be added to any package.

What is your rescheduling policy?

We understand that schedules can change. Appointments can be rescheduled up to 48 hours before your session without any fee. Last-minute changes may incur a small rebooking fee.
